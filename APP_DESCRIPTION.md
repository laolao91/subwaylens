## Official App Description (FINAL)

**Never Miss a Train Again**

SubwayLens puts real-time NYC subway arrivals directly on your Even Realities G2 glasses. Check arrival times with a glance—no need to pull out your phone while walking, standing on a crowded platform, or juggling bags and coffee.

**What You Get**

Real-time arrival times for all NYC subway lines (1-7, A-Z, SIR) pulled directly from MTA's live data feeds. Scroll between your favorite stations with your R1 ring or temple gestures. See upcoming trains in both directions simultaneously. Borough direction codes (MAN/QNS/BK/BX) help you quickly understand where trains are heading without memorizing terminal names.

**Key Features**

• Live MTA data — No fake times, ever. When feeds are down, you'll know.
• Favorite stations — Add up to 15 stations and scroll between them instantly
• Borough codes — See MAN/QNS/BK/BX below direction headers to understand train destinations at a glance
• Nearby stations — GPS-based discovery shows subway stations within walking distance
• Auto-refresh — Configurable refresh intervals (15s to 2min) keep times current
• No backend — All data fetched directly from MTA. No third-party servers, no API keys, no accounts.
• Privacy-first — Your favorites stay on your device. No tracking, no analytics, no data collection.

**How It Works**

Configure your favorite stations on your phone (search 470+ NYC subway stations), then scroll between them on your glasses. Tap to manually refresh, double-tap to exit. The app fetches live GTFS-RT feeds directly from api-endpoint.mta.info and displays upcoming arrivals for both directions.

**Perfect For**

NYC subway riders who want hands-free access to real-time arrival information while commuting, traveling with luggage, or navigating the city.

Built by a NYC subway rider, for NYC subway riders. Open source under GPLv3.
